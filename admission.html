PRESCHOOL ADMISSION

Qualifications

Toddler – Two (2) years old by October of the current school year
Pre-nursery – Three (3) years old by October of the current school year
Nursery – Four (4) years old by October of the current school year
Kinder – Five (5) years old by October of the current school year

General Instructions to Parents

A. For Assessment

Kindly prepare the following requirements and email to info@johndewey.edu.ph before the deadline of application.
Accomplished online assessment and application forms. Type in BLOCK LETTERS.
Digital image of applicant’s most recent 1½ x 1½ colored picture
Scanned copy of the applicant’s original National Statistics Office (NSO) birth or adoption certificate (for validation only)
Pay the online assessment fee. (The assessment details such as Zoom meeting ID, password, schedule, etc. will be provided to you.)

B. For Enrollment

Accomplish and submit the online Student Information form. Type in BLOCK LETTERS. Be sure to include the scanned copy of the applicant’s recent Medical Certificate.
*Additional Requirements for Transferees Latest Progress Report or Report Card
Recommendation Letter from the Class Adviser, Guidance Counselor, and/or Principal

*Foreign Applicants

Copy of alien certificate
Copy of passport pages showing: Applicant and parent/s picture, date of birth, passport number, date of issue and expiration date
Visa showing length of stay in the Philippines
Application Schedule Assessment (new students) – by appointment
Enrollment Schedule Continuing students – December to April
New students – Ongoing throughout the school year provided slots are available
Please apply as soon as possible because these deadlines will be changed as soon as the quota is reached.
IMPORTANT:Incomplete application forms will not be processed. Applicant will be scheduled for assessment a week after the completion of the requirements. Please update the office of any changes in your contact numbers. Results of the assessment will be released during the scheduled interview of the applicant and the parents.
